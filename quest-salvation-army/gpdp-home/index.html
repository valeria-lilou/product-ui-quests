The Salvation Army Grants Per Diem (GPD) program offers Veterans an opportunity for a fresh start in life.

For people without homes, the program provides temporary shelter in a safe, healthy atmosphere, and facilitates their pursuit of sustainable self-sufficiency.

GPD Housing Program Application

Eligible Veterans:

  • Served on active duty
  • Do not have a disqualifying discharge
  • Are unhoused or at risk
  • Agree to participate in the program's structure and goals
